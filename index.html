About This Bread Collection

Each loaf, bun, and roll in this collection is homemade with care. From savory swirls filled with bacon and cheese to delicate matcha cream pastries, every piece tells a small story of warmth and comfort.

Bread is more than food — it’s texture, fragrance, and memory baked into golden crusts. These photos capture the playful variety of Bekah’s kitchen experiments, sometimes soft and fluffy, sometimes rich and hearty, always inviting.

  • Soft loaves with unique fillings
  • Sweet and savory buns
  • Creative character breads
  • A gallery of flavors, colors, and textures
